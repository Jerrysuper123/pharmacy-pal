Get me a nearest pharmacy

Pharmacy-pal is designed to aid "self-care through pharmacy" in the midst of Covid19, especially one is feeling unwell.

You could easily access the nearby pharmacy location through this app, together with the registered pharmacist in service.

We hope to give you the same peace of mind when visiting clinics/hospitals, strained by covid19, has become less accessible.

Our drug advisor feature also allows you to do symptom checking & diagnosis and recommends appropriate medications.

Please note that our recommendations are for reference ONLY and consult your doctors/pharmacist before taking any medications.

An adverse event (drug side-effects) is submitted to the US FDA to report any undesirable experience associated with the use of a drug, including serious drug side effects, product use errors, product quality problems, and therapeutic failures.

Reporting of adverse events by healthcare professionals and consumers is voluntary. News, enforcement actions, and other phenomena can also spur reporting.
